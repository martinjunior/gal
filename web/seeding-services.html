Glen Ellyn, Seeding and Sodding Services

Residential Lawn Seeding Services

We use healthy sod from local farms. Great American Landscaping provides full seeding and soding services, all you have to do is water! We offer multiple types of seeds and sods, but, don't fret, our professionals will guide you in picking the sod or seeds that are right for your lawn. Some people ask, "Isn't grass, just grass?" The answer is no. There are many grass types and some may be more suitable for your lawn than others. Seeding generally offers more grass types, while sod is quick and easy. Seeding is a longer process, because old grass or weeds must be removed before seeding begins. Plus, seed takes time to grow. Both options, seeding and sodding, yield beautiful results. That choice is yours; Great American Landscaping specializes in both residential seeding and sodding.
